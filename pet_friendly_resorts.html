Top 10 Pet-Friendly Resorts in Bangalore

01

Suggee Resort

Suggee Resort is a pet-friendly getaway surrounded by greenery and wide-open spaces where pets can roam safely. The resort offers pet-welcoming cottages, shaded walking trails, and play lawns, making it ideal for guests travelling with dogs or cats. Water bowls and basic pet assistance are available on request.

02

Whispering Palms Resort

Whispering Palms Resort warmly welcomes pets and features large lawns, peaceful walking paths and open garden seating. Pets can accompany you outdoors while families relax. Designated pet zones and outdoor dining areas ensure a comfortable stay for both owners and pets.

Get price
03

Nandi Breeze Resort

Nandi Breeze Resort near Nandi Hills is suitable for pet parents who enjoy long walks and fresh air with their pets. The resort has open landscapes, garden trails and easy outdoor access from many rooms, keeping pets active and stress free during the holiday.

Get price
04

Sandalwood Grove Retreat

Sandalwood Grove Retreat is a pet-friendly nature resort that allows pets inside select cottages and outdoor areas. With greenery, safe walking tracks and minimal internal traffic, it provides a calm and secure environment for pets and their families to unwind together.

Get price
05

Greenstone Retreat

Greenstone Retreat welcomes pets and offers landscaped lawns, stone pathways and shaded seating ideal for pet walks. The quiet ambience is especially helpful for anxious pets. The resort team supports simple needs such as warm water, food heating and bedding on request.

Get price
06

Royal Fern Retreat

Royal Fern Retreat is designed for guests travelling with pets, surrounded by fern gardens and natural trails. Pets are permitted in outdoor areas and certain accommodation types. Leash-friendly paths and spacious open grounds make it easy to enjoy nature time with your pets.

Get price
07

Misty Banyan Retreat

Misty Banyan Retreat is a countryside pet-friendly resort with tree-lined avenues and natural trails. Pets can stay close to owners in cottage verandas and outdoor seating spaces, making it an excellent pick for relaxed breaks with furry companions.

Get price
08

Peacock Ridge Resort

Peacock Ridge Resort is pet-friendly and set amidst wide ridge-view landscapes. Pets can enjoy long, scenic walks along open pathways while families relax in cottage stays. Pet bedding may be provided based on availability.

Get price
09

Lotus Valley Hideaway

Lotus Valley Hideaway welcomes pets and offers quiet, leash-friendly walking paths around lotus ponds and green fields. The peaceful environment suits both energetic pets and senior pets that prefer slow strolls and restful surroundings.

10

Blue Mist Retreat

Blue Mist Retreat is a pet-friendly resort offering cottage accommodations, cool breezes and open play areas. Pets are allowed in designated outdoor spaces and selected room categories so families can enjoy vacations without leaving pets at home.
