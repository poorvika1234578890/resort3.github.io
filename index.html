Top 10 Resorts in Bangalore

01

Suggee Resort

Suggee Resort, located near Anekal (Bengaluru), is a sprawling 12-acre nature and adventure escape offering thrilling activities like zip-lining, sky-cycling, and a large bird aviary, alongside cosy cottages, tents and huts nestled in mango orchards. It’s ideal for families, groups or corporate retreats seeking fun plus tranquillity just outside the city.

Sandalwood Grove Retreat

Sandalwood Grove Retreat is a serene nature escape inspired by Karnataka’s iconic sandalwood forests, offering a warm, earthy, and aromatic ambiance. With lush garden pathways and tranquil villa stays, it’s the perfect spot for a peaceful Bangalore getaway.

Greenstone Retreat

Greenstone Retreat is a peaceful nature getaway located on the green outskirts of Bangalore. Designed with stone-clad cottages and lush landscaped gardens, the resort offers a quiet, cozy atmosphere perfect for relaxation.

Royal Fern Retreat

Royal Fern Retreat is a peaceful nature escape set amid lush fern gardens on Bangalore’s green outskirts. With cozy stone-and-wood cottages and serene walking paths, it offers a refreshing break from city life. A perfect spot for quiet stays, nature lovers, and weekend relaxation.

Misty Banyan Retreat

Misty Banyan Retreat blends misty morning views with the charm of ancient banyan trees, creating a peaceful nature escape near Bangalore. Cozy cottages, quiet garden paths, and a calm countryside atmosphere make it perfect for a relaxing getaway.

Peacock Ridge Resort

Peacock Ridge Resort sits along a scenic countryside ridge where peacocks are often seen roaming freely, offering a natural and vibrant escape near Bangalore. With hill-view cottages and serene open landscapes, it’s perfect for a peaceful weekend retreat.
